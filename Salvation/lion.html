Follow the King of Kings

The Lion of Judah calls you to victory. This is your moment of triumph.

Are you ready to surrender to the King of Kings and claim victory over death?

The Lion of Judah has conquered sin and death. He stands ready to make you more than a conqueror. Today, you can join His eternal kingdom and know with absolute certainty that you have victory in Christ.

The King Himself gives us the path to victory. His Word declares:

"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you will be saved. For it is within your heart that you believe and are justified, and it is with your mouth that you profess your faith and are saved."
Romans 10:9-10 (NIV)

The Lion of Judah has already won the battle. All you must do is surrender to His lordship and claim the victory He has secured for you.

Declare your surrender to the King with this prayer:

"Almighty God, I bow before You as the King of Kings and Lord of Lords. I acknowledge that Jesus Christ, the Lion of Judah, conquered sin and death for me. I surrender my life to His lordship and accept His victory as my own. Jesus, You are my King, my Savior, and my Champion. I believe You died for my sins, rose from the dead, and now reign in heaven. Today I claim Your victory and commit to serve You as my Lord for all eternity. In the mighty name of Jesus, Amen."

Victory is yours! You are now a warrior in the army of the King of Kings!

You have made the greatest decision of your life. The Lion of Judah has claimed you as His own, and nothing can separate you from His love and victory.

Your Victory March Begins:

As a warrior for Christ:

  • Study God's Word daily (start with the Gospel of John) - your sword of the Spirit
  • Find a strong church family to stand with you in battle
  • Communicate with your King through prayer - He is always listening
  • Share your victory with others who need to know the King
  • Get baptized as a public declaration of your allegiance to Christ
