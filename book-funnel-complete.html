1
Visitor Lands on Book Page
Someone interested in learning about AI and faith discovers your book page. They see compelling information about "The Necessary Evil" and the valuable free resources you're offering.
📋 What They See:
  • Book Overview: Description of "The Necessary Evil" and its message
  • Free Offer: Christian AI Survival Kit valued at $71
  • 4 PDF Guides: Preview of the resources they'll receive
  • Simple Form: Easy signup to get instant access
  • Trust Signals: Professional design and clear value proposition
2
They Sign Up with Their Email
The visitor decides the free resources are valuable and enters their first name, last name, and email address. The moment they click "Sign Up," the automated system springs into action.
⚡ What Happens Instantly (Behind the Scenes):
  • Data Saved: Their information is securely stored in the subscriber database
  • Admin Alert: You receive an email notification about the new signup
  • Welcome Email Triggered: System prepares to send PDFs immediately
  • Drip Enrollment: They're automatically added to the 7-email series
  • Access Granted: They can now access the download page anytime
3
Welcome Email Arrives (Day 0)
Within seconds, they receive a personalized welcome email addressed to them by their first name. This email contains everything they need to get started with the resources.
📦 What's Included in the Email:
  • Personal Greeting: "Hey [First Name]," - makes it feel personal
  • 4 PDF Attachments: All guides attached directly to the email
  • Christian AI Survival Guide: The main comprehensive resource
  • Church Discussion Guide: For group conversations and study
  • AI Parent Guide: Helping families navigate AI safely
  • Download Page Link: Access resources anytime in the future
  • Preference Links: Manage email settings or unsubscribe
4
Automated 7-Email Series Begins
Over the next 2 weeks, the subscriber receives 6 additional educational emails. Each email is carefully timed and personalized, providing valuable insights about AI, faith, and practical action steps. All emails are sent automatically at 9am EST on their scheduled days.
📅 Complete Email Timeline:
📧 Day 1
Your First Step with AI - Getting started with understanding
📧 Day 3
Understanding AI Bias - How algorithms shape perspectives
📧 Day 5
Protecting Your Family - Practical safety measures
📧 Day 7
Church & Community - Leading conversations
📧 Day 10
Taking Action - Practical steps forward
📧 Day 14
Final Thoughts - Continuing the journey
✨ What Makes This Special:
  • Personalized: Every email uses their first name
  • Perfectly Timed: Spaced for optimal engagement and learning
  • Educational Journey: Builds knowledge progressively
  • 100% Automated: You don't have to do anything
  • Professional Delivery: Sent through reliable email system
5
Subscriber Has Full Control
Every email includes links at the bottom that give subscribers complete control over their experience. They can manage their preferences, pause the series, or opt out entirely - all with just a click.
