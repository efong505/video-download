Foreword by Ethan Lozoya
Systems & IT Professional: "Edward's book is a wake-up call every Christian needs in 2026. AI is a tool — but only under God's authority."

AI is here. It is powerful. It is dangerous. And it is unavoidable.

In less than one month, I built an entire Christian-conservative media platform using AI — something that would have taken a team years. But when the same tool refused to tell the truth about real events unless labeled "hypothetical," I uncovered something far more troubling: AI is not neutral. It is biased, ideologically slanted, and accelerating a transhumanist vision that seeks to redefine humanity itself.

This is not a book against technology.
It is a book about mastering it — before it masters us.
