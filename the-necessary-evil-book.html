Early Reader Testimonial

About the Author

Edward Fong is a cloud-native systems engineer, platform architect, and founder of ChristianConservativesToday.com.

With degrees from New Mexico State University and experience supporting secure government environments at Kirtland Air Force Base under an active DoD Secret clearance, Edward designs and operates production-grade AWS serverless platforms using Infrastructure as Code (Terraform), CI/CD automation, blue/green deployment strategies, and disciplined monitoring practices.

In 2025, he built and launched an entire Christian-conservative media platform in under one month using artificial intelligence — an effort that would traditionally have required a full development team. That experience gave him firsthand exposure to both the extraordinary productivity gains AI enables and the subtle ways these systems shape information, narrative framing, and cultural direction.

The Necessary Evil was written not as a reactionary critique of technology, but as a technical and theological examination of AI's accelerating influence. Drawing from direct experience building and operating AI-enabled systems, Edward explores the structural biases embedded in modern platforms, the transhumanist assumptions driving technological development, and the spiritual implications of delegating judgment to machines.

The Second Edition (February 2026) expands on media manipulation, governance trends, and the responsibility of the Church to engage technology with clarity, courage, and discipline.

Edward lives in Albuquerque, New Mexico, with his wife Julia, an artist and graphic designer who contributes visual elements to their shared ministry and creative work.
