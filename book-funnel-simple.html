📚 How Our Book Email System Works

A simple guide to understanding the automated email journey

📊 Visual Flowchart 📖 Simple Guide 📚 Complete Guide 🔧 Technical Diagram
1
🌐
Someone Visits the Book Page
A person interested in the book lands on our website. They see information about "The Necessary Evil" book and the free Christian AI Survival Kit worth $71.
📋 What They See:
  • Book description and benefits
  • Free Survival Kit offer (4 PDF guides)
  • Preview of what's inside
  • Email signup form to get the free resources
↓
2
✍️
They Sign Up with Their Email
The visitor enters their name and email address to receive the free PDF resources. As soon as they click "Sign Up," several things happen automatically.
⚡ What Happens Instantly:
  • Their information is saved to our subscriber list
  • They receive a welcome email with all 4 PDF guides attached
  • You (the admin) get notified about the new signup
  • They're automatically enrolled in the 7-email educational series
  • They can access the download page anytime
↓
3
📧
They Receive Welcome Email (Day 0)
Within seconds of signing up, they get a personalized welcome email with their name. The email includes all 4 PDF resources as attachments and a link to download them anytime.
📦 What's Included:
  • Christian AI Survival Guide - Main resource
  • Church Discussion Guide - For group conversations
  • AI Parent Guide - Protecting families
  • Additional Resources - Bonus materials
  • Link to manage their email preferences
↓
4
📅
Automated Email Series Begins
Over the next 2 weeks, they automatically receive 6 more helpful emails. Each email is personalized with their first name and provides valuable information about AI and faith. The system sends these emails at 9am EST on scheduled days.

5
⚙️
They Can Manage Their Preferences
Every email includes links at the bottom so subscribers can control what they receive. They have full control over their subscription.
🎛️ Subscriber Options:
  • Pause emails - Take a break from the series
  • Resume emails - Continue where they left off
  • Manage preferences - Choose what types of emails to receive
  • Unsubscribe - Stop all emails if they want
  • Re-download PDFs - Access resources anytime
↓
6
📊
You Track Everything
As the admin, you have a dashboard where you can see all subscriber activity, track the email series progress, and manage your list. You get notified of new signups and can resend emails if needed.
👀 What You Can See:
  • Total number of subscribers
  • Who's actively receiving emails
  • Where each person is in the 7-email series
  • Who has paused or unsubscribed
  • Ability to resend welcome emails
  • Real-time analytics and metrics

The Simple Version
Think of it like this: When someone signs up for your book resources, they're like a guest checking into a hotel. They immediately get their room key (welcome email with PDFs), and then over the next 2 weeks, the hotel staff (automated system) delivers helpful information to their door at specific times. They can call the front desk anytime (manage preferences) to change their service, and you (the hotel manager) can see who's checked in and how their stay is going. Everything happens automatically, so you don't have to manually send each email or remember who needs what. The system does it all for you!
